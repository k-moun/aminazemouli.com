July 2024

3D printed hand brace

I participated in a challenge for 3D modeling a hand brace. It was organized by a company that scans hands of patients with an injury or pain in their hands then 3D models a suitable brace for them. The brace is obviously too big for my hand because it was modeled based on a patient's hand. It was a great challenge. I worked with SUBD in Rhino to make the shape based on the hand then I used a voronoi script in Grasshopper to shape it in a way that it becomes breathable. It looks quite cool
